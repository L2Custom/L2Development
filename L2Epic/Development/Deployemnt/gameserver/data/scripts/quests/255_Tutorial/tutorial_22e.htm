[Elven Knight Class Transfer]:
An Elven Knight who reaches level 40 can proceed with the 2nd class transfer to become a Temple Knight or a Swordsinger. In order to accomplish the 2nd class transfer, a player must successfully complete three quests. The first of the three quests can only be undertaken by a character of level 35 or above. If you would like to view more detailed information, please click the class you are interested in.

If you do not want to make the quest, you have some additiona options:

Buy quest item: You may also buy the quest item from Caius for 700.000 Adena each.It can be found in Gludin, Gludio and Giran

Direct transfer: At level 40, tutorial mark will apear and you can chouse the disierd class at the cost of 2.100.000 Adena. Note: if you chouse this option, you will not recive shadow weapon exchange cupons (C)
